    IAO Textual Entity¶

    A textual entity (IAO_0000300) must always be about something via iao:is_about.

    TTL Editor
    Validation Results

    About This Example¶

    This example uses the Information Artifact Ontology (IAO) to model a publication that references a dataset. The SHACL constraint ensures every textual entity has at least one is_about relation.

    Try It¶

    Click Edit & Validate and try removing the iao:is_about triple to trigger a constraint violation.
